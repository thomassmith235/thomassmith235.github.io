FMEA / FMECA for wind assets

Prioritize wind reliability with a structured FMEA workflow.

Map turbines, score risk, and share clean reports. The FMEA tool keeps onshore and offshore assets organized with severity-occurrence-detection scoring and export-ready outputs.

Highlights

Asset hierarchy that matches your turbines

Model towers, blades, drivetrains, and electrical subsystems with variant-specific fields for offshore foundations and inter-array cabling.

Fast risk scoring

Severity, occurrence, and detection fields recalc RPN instantly, with filters to keep the highest risk items at the top.

Collaboration ready

Save projects to the backend, reopen, and export for OEM submissions, regulators, or service partners without rework.

Workflow
01

Authenticate

Request a token from /api/auth/login, then sign in via the UI. Swap in your deployed URL to point at production.

02

Build the FMEA

Add subsystems and components, capture failure modes, effects, and mitigations, and score S/O/D with instant RPN updates.

03

Share the outputs

Export CSV/PDF for audits and service crews, or reopen projects later to keep the risk register current.
