FMECA tool for wind assets

Plan wind turbine maintenance with a structured FMECA workflow.

Capture subsystems, components, and failure modes, score them with severity-occurrence-detection, and export clean reports. Backed by a Django API and a focused React UI built for turbine operations.

What you can do

Model the hierarchy

Capture subsystems and components, then add failure modes with effects and mitigation notes. Everything stays organized by the assets you maintain.

Score risk fast

Use 1-10 scoring for severity, occurrence, and detection. RPN is calculated instantly so you can focus on the riskiest work.

Save, reload, export

Persist projects to the Django API, reopen them with a click, and export to CSV or PDF for audits and handoffs.

How it works
01

Authenticate

Request a token from the API endpoint (/api/auth/login) and sign in with your email and token.

02

Build your FMECA

Add subsystems, components, and failure modes. Capture effects, score S/O/D, and watch the RPN update as you type.

03

Save and share

Save to the backend, reload projects later, and export to CSV or PDF for reviews, OEM submissions, or service crews.
